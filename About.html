About Me

I'm a passionate tech enthusiast from Sri Lanka, currently studying in my second year at IIT University. My journey began at Rahula College Matara, where I worked as a technician in the school media unit and engaged with the ICT club, which ignited my love for technology. I specialize in front-end development and have a keen interest in artificial intelligence and machine learning. Through hands-on projects and participation in tech events, I continuously expand my knowledge of programming languages and emerging technologies. My goal is to combine my skills to create innovative, user-centered solutions that make a meaningful impact.
